Masala Chai (Indian Tea)

Thandi ke mausam me garma-garam chai!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Water (Paani)
  • 1 cup Milk (Doodh)
  • 2 tsp Tea Leaves (Chai Patti)
  • 1 inch Ginger (Adrak)
  • 2 Cardamom (Elaichi)
  • Sugar to taste (Cheeni)

Nutrition Facts

Nutrient Amount
Calories 120 kcal
Fat 4g
Sugar 10g

Instructions

  1. Paani ko ubaalein (Boil water).
  2. Adrak aur Elaichi ko koot kar daalein.
  3. Chai patti aur cheeni daalein.
  4. Doodh daalein aur 2-3 ubaal aane dein.
  5. Chaan kar garma-garam serve karein!
